Mindset rules

Soft discipline. Athlete energy.

💌
Romanticise the process Cute outfit, good playlist, water bottle, post-workout coffee. Make the routine feel like you.
🧠
Discipline over drama You do not need to feel inspired. You only need to start.
🔥
Fatigue is part of Hyrox Train smart under fatigue, but do not confuse toughness with never recovering.
🌱
Protect the long game A deload week is not weakness. It is how you come back stronger.
